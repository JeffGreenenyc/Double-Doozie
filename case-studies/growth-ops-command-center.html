Growth Ops Command Center

A single executive view that synthesizes funnel health, lifecycle segments, churn risk, channel truth, and promo lift into one operating rhythm.

Currently in development
Real data. Real charts. Coming Q4 2026.

Most exec dashboards are noise. This one is a deliberate capstone — once Funnel Leak, Lifecycle Segmentation, Churn Early Warning, Channel Truth, and Promo Lift are live, the Command Center stitches their KPIs into a single weekly operating view for a growth leader.

Intentionally last · this dashboard pulls from every other case study, so they have to exist first

  • North-star KPI tree: revenue · acquisition quality · retention · LTV · CAC payback
  • Funnel health snapshot (sourced from Funnel Leak Finder)
  • Segment movement (sourced from Lifecycle Segmentation)
  • Churn risk roll-up (sourced from Churn Early Warning model)
  • Channel mix + promo ROI tiles (sourced from Channel Truth + Promo Lift)
  • Designed for a 30-minute weekly growth review — the page a CMO or CRO actually opens
