Channel Truth Dashboard

Cuts through attribution noise to show which channels actually drive revenue and quality customers.

Currently in development
Real data. Real charts. Coming Q3 2026.

A governed marketing dashboard that ties spend to revenue with a consistent channel taxonomy, a transparent attribution heuristic, and cohort quality layered on top — so budget decisions stop being arguments and start being measurable.

Planning complete · dataset selected · build starts after Churn Early Warning ships
What this case study will show
  • Unified channel taxonomy (UTM/source mapping) across paid and organic
  • Last non-direct attribution within a 7-day window — explainable, not magical
  • Primary KPI: MER/ROAS · Secondary: CAC, revenue per acquired customer (60–90 day)
  • Cohort quality overlay so high-ROAS-but-low-retention channels get flagged
  • Weekly ops view + monthly strategy view in a single Power BI report
